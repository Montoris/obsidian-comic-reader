Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Obsidian Comic Reader does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

Your files

Comic files you import are stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, your files are stored in your own iCloud Drive under your Apple Account. The developer has no access to your files or your iCloud data.

Reading data

Reading progress and library organization (collections, read/unread status) are stored locally on your device and never leave it.

Third parties

The app contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party trackers. Nothing about your usage is sent to the developer or anyone else.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data at all, it collects no data from children. The app ships no content of its own; it only displays the files you choose to import.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date above.
